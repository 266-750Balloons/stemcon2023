The Innards of Computing, Quick and Easy(ish)

CPU computes the big stuff, GPU the graphics, Motherboard glues everything together, RAM is short-term storage

For the most part, that is true. When getting deeper, not as many people will think of the littler bits (I'll get into that later.).

This interactive micro-course will go over the following concpets and (hopefully) bring them down to simple terms:
