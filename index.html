The Innards of Computing, Quick and Easy(ish)

To many people, computers seem like absolute magic.

When more savvy people are asked about how these essential machines work, most would probably give a rehash of the following:

CPU computes the big stuff, GPU the graphics, Motherboard glues everything together, RAM is short-term storage

For the most part, that is true. When getting deeper, not as many people will think of the littler bits (I'll get into that later.).

This micro-course will go over the following concepts and (hopefully) bring them down to simple terms:

  • Transtitors
  • Logic Gates
  • Binary

You can click on the sidebar to navigate to each section. I recommend going through them in order so you can play with the binary toy at the end.
